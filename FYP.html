Final Year Project


"IoT Based Waste Management System Using Real Time Database"


Introduction

From Electronic and Telecommunication Engineering background and interested in doing embedded projects and analyzing data through Data Science concept, I thought of making something useful for the society. A major problem in day to day life is waste management especially the garbage bins maintenance. This issue indirectly leads to a physical and mental problem for the society. My plane to make a device that can make the waste management jobs very easy. This project presents the basic concept of using server or cloud-based smart Garbage bin in smart cities as an important application of the Internet of Things. This system will be accessible through a mobile application or through the webpage and can be used to monitor and find the empty and filled bins in that area.
